Housing Information

Welcome to Housing Services

Finding suitable housing is crucial for a successful university experience. Below you will find important information regarding housing options, application processes, and support services.

Housing Options

  • On-Campus Housing: Our campus offers a variety of residence halls with different amenities and styles. Each residence is equipped with study spaces and common areas.
  • Off-Campus Housing: Explore nearby apartments and shared accommodations. A list of approved housing providers is available to assist your search.

Application Process

To apply for housing, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online housing application on the university portal.
  2. Submit the required documents, including proof of enrollment and any requested financial information.
  3. Wait for confirmation of your housing assignment via email.
