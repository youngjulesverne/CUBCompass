Course Registration

Welcome to Course Registration

Registering for courses is an essential part of your academic journey. Follow the steps below to ensure you successfully enroll in the courses you need.

Steps to Register

  1. Log in to your student portal.
  2. Select the 'Course Registration' option from the menu.
  3. Browse available courses for the semester.
  4. Add desired courses to your registration cart.
  5. Review your selections and confirm your registration.
  6. Pay any necessary fees to complete the registration process.

Important Dates

  • Registration Opens: January 15
  • Registration Closes: January 31
  • Add/Drop Period: February 1 - February 7
